Japanese Anime

Japanese anime is a big part of the country. More than millions of Japanese people watch anime everyday. Anime was originated from Japan and it's popularity has been increasing year after year. Anime is the Japanese term for "Animation". It is a word used to describe cartoons or animations produced within Japan. Anime has been around for many years, which has created an incredible fanbase. Some famous anime characters include Naruto, Goku, itachi, Luffy, etc. Some famous animes include One Punch Man, Naruto, Attack On Titan, Dragon Ball, etc. Anime is so popular that it is even considered a genre everywhere.




There are more than thousands of types of animes in Japan which captured so people's atttention to watch them. Since anime has such a large fan base around the world, people are starting to make english subtitles or english versions of the animes. It can be accessed in many different ways such as websites, televisions, phones, and even theatres.



Some of the scenes created in animes actually exist in real life. For example, in the anime movie, "Your Name", a scene was created based on a small town called Itomori in Japan. Because of the anime scenes that are created based on real places, visitors often want to go visit them.
